The Rules · For When You're Tired
01
Say the specific thing, not the general thing. Not "honour your body." The jaw click in the morning. The specific detail is the portal.
02
One idea per post. One door, opened fully, is more useful than five doors ajar.
03
Don't explain what the reader can feel. Trust them to arrive there.
04
No Delta BC. No specific neighbourhood. Vancouver, the coast, outside — that's enough.
05
Don't perform wellness. The moment the writing sounds like it has everything figured out — stop. Go back to what's actually true right now.
06
Returning is the practice, not consistency. Miss a term. Come back. That's the whole philosophy applied to itself.
07
Write to her. She's in a season where the body is asking more of her than it used to. She's not looking for someone who has it figured out. She wants someone honest about being in the middle of it. You are that person.
