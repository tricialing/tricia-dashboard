The Whole Idea
The mother who just gave birth has a newborn with colic and a partner running on empty and her own nervous system that never got a break. Treat all three. Listening Season is how people find you before they need you. The clinic is where it all lands.

04 Workshops 2028 onwards
Seasonal

One per season, not per solar term. Enough depth to go deep. Spring workshop feels different from Autumn. People return each year and feel the shift in their own bodies.
Specialty
Prenatal & Postpartum
TCM care for the fourth trimester and beyond. You lived this. The home birth, the bodywork, the first year of motherhood. Personal conviction + clinical training.
05 UX TCM · The Clinic 2030
Practice Philosophy
The Family as a Unit
Treat the mother, the child, the partner. The family system, not the isolated individual. Like your chiropractor — everyone is cared for. TCM has always thought in systems.
Specialty 1
Birth Work
Prenatal, postpartum, fourth trimester. Your personal story is your clinical conviction. Almost nobody doing this in a way that feels human rather than clinical or woo.
Specialty 2
ADHD & TCM
The nervous system, inattention, chronic holding, the jaw. You're not theorising — you're researching your own body. Almost no one doing this seriously yet.
The Loop
Observation Log → Intake
The free printable from 2026 becomes the body literacy training your patients already have when they walk in. They know how to answer "how are you feeling."
Future Build
Observation Log App
The printable becomes a proper body tracking app built around the 24 solar terms and TCM organ systems. Seasonal check-ins, energy and mood tracking, pattern recognition over time. Doesn't exist yet. You have the clinical depth, the design brain, and the tech fluency to build it.

LS Audience → Warm Referrals
People who found you in 2026 become your first patients in 2030. The brand and the practice are the same thing.
