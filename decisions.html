Voice & Writing
Voice
No em dashes. Ever.
Not Tricia's voice. Real writing samples (Just Noodling newsletters) had zero em dashes. Use commas, full stops, or new sentences instead.
Voice
No pull quotes.
Tricia skips pull quotes when reading. If something is important, make it a bold paragraph or a standalone line instead.
Voice
References go in the body, not as name-drops.
Miranda July stays as a quality description ("the writer who can spend three paragraphs on something ordinary...") not as a name-drop lead. The feeling, not the credential.
Voice
Curiosity is the thread. Not suffering, not crisis.
The origin story is about genuine interest in the body and TCM being something that was always there, deepened by the birth experience. TMJD and ADHD are not the origin story. They are craft material for specific content, not the brand's foundation.
Voice
Audience is curiosity-led, not crisis-led.
"She is not necessarily unwell. She is curious. She feels something — about her energy, her cycles, her mood, the way her body shifts with the seasons — and she wants to understand it better." Changed from earlier framing that led with pain.

Brand
Listening Season and the future clinic are the same project.
The Observation Log is not just content. It is the solution to the UX problem Tricia identified (patients arrive having never been taught to notice). The brand builds the audience. The audience becomes the patients. 2026 to 2030 is one arc.
UX TCM · Clinic Design
UX TCM
The core problem: patients arrive unprepared and leave untaught.
Nobody tells patients what to notice before an appointment, so they arrive with "here hurts, I'm tired." Nobody explains why a pattern developed, so they leave treated but not educated. The relationship stays transactional.
UX TCM
Goal: patients leave knowing something about their body they didn't know before.
Body literacy. They can read the signs, explain what happened in the session, and arrive better prepared next time. A take-home (Observation Log + session notes) is part of this.
UX TCM
This is a total experience overhaul, online and in person.
Lighting, sound, atmosphere. Every touchpoint designed, not accumulated. The Observation Log is the pre-appointment tool. The take-home is the post-appointment tool. The space itself is designed to feel good and beautiful.
