The One Rule
If it crossed your mind —
send it to WhatsApp → Capacities.
One sentence. Right now. No formatting.
01
Capture in the moment · zero friction
While cooking, before sleep, whenever. Don't judge it. Don't format it. Don't decide where it goes. Just send it.
WhatsApp → Capacities Daily Note
02
Sort twice a week · 15 minutes
Open last few days of Daily Notes. For each capture: which solar term? Still alive? Move to Field Note. That's the whole job, no writing, just filing.
Daily Notes → Field Notes → Solar Term page
03
Make when something has weight
Open current Solar Term page. Look at Alive notes. If one keeps pulling at you, that's the signal. Ask: caption, photo, or something longer?

What a capture looks like
One sentence only. What you noticed + what it made you feel or think.
No tags, no hashtags, no formatting. That comes at sort time.
Voice note is fine. Capacities will receive it. Transcribe at sort time.
