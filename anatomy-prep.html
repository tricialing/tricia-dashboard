Anatomy Prep · Curriculum
Mar – Sep 1 2026 15 units · 57 modules · CMU OLI School: Sep 8 2026

CMU OLI Anatomy & Physiology — free, self-paced, 15 units, 57 modules. One unit per week is comfortable. At that pace you finish by late May, well ahead of September.

The goal is not mastery. It is to arrive at school with a working mental map of the body so the first semester clicks instead of overwhelms. Reading, Shu point work, and coloring book pages are woven in alongside — a little each unit, not all at once.
