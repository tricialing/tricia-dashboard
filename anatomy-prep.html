Anatomy Prep · Curriculum
Feb 16 – Sep 1 2026 26 weeks · 1 session/week School: Sep 8 2026

CMU OLI Anatomy & Physiology — free, self-paced, two-semester equivalent. One session per week. You have exactly the time the course was designed for.

The goal is not to master everything. It is to arrive at school with a working mental map of the body so the first semester clicks instead of overwhelms. Reading and Shu point work is folded in alongside the CMU modules — do a little each session, not all at once.
