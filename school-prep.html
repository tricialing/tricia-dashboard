days until first day of school · Sep 8 2026
The real story
A mother. An ADHD brain. A brand new field. Starting school at 38 with a toddler and a content brand. The people following Listening Season are watching someone do the thing — not explain the thing. This preparation is the content.
Admin — things to think through before September
Confirm enrolment and what steps remain
Sep 8 2026 is the target. Is your spot confirmed? What paperwork is outstanding?
Apply for student loans — BC and federal
No interest while in school. Opens through StudentAidBC in spring before your start year. Use them — that is what they are for.
Research bursaries and scholarships for mature students
Many go unclaimed. Check the school directly, CTCMA, and BC health profession foundations. A mother returning to school is a compelling profile.
Map the actual schedule and find the childcare gaps
Accelerated — 3 semesters per year. When are classes? When are clinics? What happens on evenings or Saturdays? June is in daycare but what shifts?
Plan the childcare adjustment for when school starts
June will be 3. What does the current daycare cover? What changes in September? Talk to T about how the household shifts.
Get a sense of textbooks and materials ahead of time
Ask the school or current students. Buy second-hand where possible. Budget for supplies separately from tuition.
Set up a study system before school starts — not during
ADHD plus an accelerated program needs structure built for your brain. Spaced repetition for memorisation. Capacities for notes. Figure this out in summer, not week one of S1.
Talk to someone who has done the accelerated program
What surprised them? What do they wish they had done before starting? What is the actual workload like in S1?
Walk T through the full shape of what is coming
Not just "school starts in September." The semesters, the clinic hours, the exam schedule, the arc to R.Ac in 2028. He needs to see it to support it.
Content — the preparation as story
Write the "why TCM" piece — the real version
Not the polished origin story. The actual reason. What drew you here. What you hope it becomes. This is your first anchor piece.
Start documenting the before
What life looks like now, before school changes everything. Daily rhythms, June's age, where you are living, what the brand looks like. Future you will want this. So will your audience.
Introduce the school arc publicly on Listening Season
Let your audience in. You do not have to share everything — but taking them into the anticipation builds the narrative before S1 even starts.
Research what TCM fundamentals you can start learning now
Five elements, meridians, the solar terms through a TCM lens. Learning it publicly is content. Starting before school is a head start and a story.
Build the ADHD going back to school angle
Fresh diagnosis. Accelerated program. First time in school as an adult who knows how her brain works. This is a real story — especially for mothers.
Plan the first day of school content moment
Sep 8 2026. What does that day look like on Listening Season? It should feel significant — because it is.
Design a sustainable study-with-me format for once school starts
Not daily. Maybe once per solar term. What you are learning, what surprises you, what connects back to the brand. Figure out the format before you need it.
Draft the school prep series as a Substack arc
4 to 6 posts from now until Sep 2026. The anticipation, the logistics, the fear, the why. Ends with first day. Just Noodling or Listening Season — your call.
