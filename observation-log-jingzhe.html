驚蟄
Awakening of Insects
March 5 – March 19, 2026
Element · Wood Organ · Liver
What this season brings
Thunder wakes the sleeping insects. Things that have been dormant begin to move. There is electricity in the air and in the body. The Liver governs the smooth flow of qi — when spring arrives, it wants to move, plan, begin. This is the season of the urge.
What to notice in your body
  • Restlessness, irritability, or sudden bursts of energy
  • Tightness in the sides, jaw, or behind the eyes
  • Strong cravings — sour foods, green things, movement
  • Dreams becoming more vivid or frequent
  • The impulse to begin things that have been waiting
The Liver in Spring
The Liver stores blood and ensures qi moves freely. When it is happy: clear vision, easy decisions, emotional flow. When it is stagnant: frustration, tight muscles, disrupted sleep. Spring is when it asks to be moved — through walking, stretching, sour flavours, and not holding things in.
One question to carry into this term
What has been waiting — in your body, in your life — for the right season to begin?
