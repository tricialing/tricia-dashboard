Unique Value Proposition

A living notebook of seasonal attention, built by someone becoming a TCM practitioner in real time. The same 24 solar terms revisited every year — the depth grows with the writer. You're not reading from the other side of the learning. You're inside it with her.

Problem · Customer
Problem

Most people feel disconnected from natural rhythms and their own bodies but don't have a framework for paying attention.

Wellness content either oversimplifies — candle-quote culture — or overwhelms with clinical theory. There is no middle voice. Someone doing the actual work, in public, without having it figured out.

Customer Segments

Women 30–45 drawn to Chinese medicine and seasonal living but not finding themselves in existing wellness content. Curious before credulous. Not looking to be fixed.

Early adopters: bicultural or diaspora background — they feel seen immediately. Broader audience arrives as the content deepens. Don't chase it — it follows naturally from school starting in September.

Solution · Channels
Solution

Instagram as the 15-day pulse. 3 posts per solar term, mix of format, 6 content streams rotating across Nature, Body, Mind, Food, Care, Story.

Just Noodling newsletter for the interior arc — monthly, personal, less resolved.

Eventually: zines, planner, paid newsletter tier, workshops. The brand becomes the clinic's warm referral network by 2030.

Channels

Instagram @listening.season — primary. Just Noodling on Substack — monthly.

Word of mouth. The audience following the whole arc becomes the referral base. No ad spend.

Listening Season newsletter — not yet. Build Instagram habit first. Probably late 2026.

Revenue · Cost
Revenue Streams
one full year cycle before monetizing

2026 — none. Building audience and showing up for one full annual cycle first.

2027 — Solar Terms Planner (paid digital/print). Seasonal zines.

2028 — Paid newsletter tier. Workshop.

2030 — Clinic. The brand audience is the patient pipeline.

No pressure to monetize early. Listening Season is a long game. Monetizing before the first full cycle would damage it.

Cost Structure

Time is the primary cost. No ad spend planned.

Tools: Canva, Substack free tier, Instagram free, Capacities for notes.

Future costs: print runs for zines, Substack paid tier revenue share, potential photography equipment.

Metrics · Unfair Advantage
Key Metrics

Instagram saves (matter more than likes). Follower growth across a full annual cycle. Newsletter open rate.

Zine pre-orders when that time comes.

Ultimately: warm referrals to the clinic in 2030.

Unfair Advantage

The arc itself. A designer with an ADHD brain and a bicultural perspective, becoming a TCM doctor as a stay-at-home mother. That combination cannot be copied.

The audience following from the beginning has skin in the story. The depth compounds every year — same 24 terms, richer each cycle.

This advantage is real but fragile. It compounds over time. Right now it is mostly potential.

Risk · What Failure Looks Like
Failure Modes
named here so they lose some of their power
01
Lost interest as the project becomes irrelevant to your own life. Protection: TCM school doesn't compete with Listening Season — it's the next chapter. The solar terms are the framework TCM uses to understand the body. You will not run out of material.
02
School intensity in S2 or S3 causes posting to drop, guilt about dropping creates distance, project goes quiet. Protection: returning is the practice, not consistency. This applies to you too. Miss a term. Come back. That's the philosophy applied to itself.
03
Pressure to monetize early damages the voice. Listening Season starts to sound like it's selling something. Protection: one full year cycle before any monetization. This is decided. Written here so future-you doesn't panic in late 2026.
04
The voice drifts toward performance — writing for the algorithm rather than for her. Protection: the content rules in content-cal.html. Re-read them when it starts to feel like work instead of noticing.
05
Boom-bust cycle hits the brand — overproducing in a good stretch, then nothing. Protection: 3 posts per term is the minimum viable move. The architecture was designed for your nervous system before you had language for why it works.
